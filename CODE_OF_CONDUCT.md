# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

NOVA is committed to fostering a welcoming, professional, and collaborative community. Great technology is built through respectful communication, constructive feedback, and mutual trust.

By participating in NOVA's projects, discussions, and community spaces, you agree to help maintain an environment that is inclusive, respectful, and focused on collaboration. This applies equally to contributors, maintainers, and anyone acting on NOVA's behalf; nobody is exempt from these standards because of their role.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Treat others with respect and professionalism.
- Welcome constructive feedback and different perspectives.
- Be patient and helpful with new contributors.
- Keep discussions technical, productive, and relevant.
- Report issues responsibly and honestly.
- Respect the privacy and security of others.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following is not tolerated in any NOVA project or community space:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Hate speech or abusive language.
- Threats or intimidation.
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Sharing confidential or private information without permission.
- Spam or promotional content unrelated to the project.
- Malicious or unauthorized security testing.

---

## Security Research

We value responsible security research. If you discover a security vulnerability, please follow our [Security Policy](./SECURITY.md) instead of opening a public Issue.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to **support@novatechnology.app**.

Reports are kept confidential. We won't share who made a report, or the details of it, beyond what's needed to look into it and respond, without that person's consent. Reports are handled by NOVA's maintainers; at NOVA's current size that means the project founder personally reviews each one.

Please include what happened, where, and, if relevant, links or screenshots. You won't be penalized for reporting in good faith, even if we conclude no action is needed.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, Issues, Pull Requests, or other interactions that violate it.

Depending on severity, actions may include:

- A private warning
- Temporary restriction from project spaces
- Permanent removal from the community
- Reporting abuse to the relevant platform, where appropriate

If you believe a decision was made unfairly, you can appeal by replying to the original notice or writing to support@novatechnology.app; a different reasonable-effort review will be given, though at NOVA's current size this can't guarantee a fully independent second reviewer.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all official NOVA spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories and Discussions
- Our [Discord community](https://discord.gg/novacont)
- Documentation
- Issue trackers and Pull Requests
- Official communication channels

---

## Questions

Questions about this Code of Conduct can be sent to **support@novatechnology.app**.
